Our Story

Cafe on Top began as a small coffee cart in Sta lucia Pasig City. Our passion for quality coffee and community quickly grew into this cozy neighborhood café.

We source our beans directly from sustainable farms and roast them in-house to bring you the freshest, most flavorful coffee experience.
